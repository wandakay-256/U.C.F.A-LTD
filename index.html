About Our Association

UNITED IN BLUE

Ultimate Chelsea Fans Association Uganda Ltd is a registered non-governmental organization (NGO) dedicated to supporting Chelsea Football Club and fostering a community of fans in Uganda.

THEME: TO BRING CHELSEA FANS TOGETHER AS ONE

MOTTO: TOGETHER WE CAN

Our mission is to promote the Chelsea spirit in Uganda, organize viewing parties and events, support local football development, and engage in community service initiatives under the Chelsea Foundation's values.
